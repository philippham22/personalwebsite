About

Since graduating from CSUEB with a Bachelors of Science in Criminal Justice and deciding not to pursue a career in law enforcement, I have been trying to find my niche. After working various jobs at a few of the tech giants in the Bay Area and not being able to maintain (contract role) a position which I would be able to grow in,I took a deep look at myself to see what I was missing. After speaking with a friend who was in a similiar shoe I was in, he told me I should learn web development
